The Eiffel Tower, Paris

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most famous landmarks in the world, located in Paris, France. It was built in 1889 and stands 324 meters tall.

The Grand Canyon, USA

The Grand Canyon is a natural wonder located in Arizona, USA. It is known for its stunning views and colorful landscape that stretches for miles.

The Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is a series of fortifications made of stone, brick, and other materials, generally built along an east-to-west line across the historical northern borders of China.
